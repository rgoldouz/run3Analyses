## Changes: 2026-01-30 (2024 light SFs with ptRel method in QCD phase space)

Merge Request: [!2](https://gitlab.cern.ch/cms-analysis-corrections/BTV/Run3-24CDEReprocessingFGHIPrompt-Summer24-NanoAODv15/-/merge_requests/2)

The 2024 preliminary SF corrections are updated to include the b-tagging SFs computed with the ptRel method in the QCD phase space. A new correction with name `UParTAK4_ptrel` is added to `btagging_preliminary.json.gz` to include the additional correction for b-jets. The name `UParTAK4_ptrel` denotes that the preliminary b-tagging SFs are derived with the ptRel method.

In the future, the combination of the `UParTAK4_kinfit` and `UParTAK4_ptrel` b-tagging SFs will be provided.
